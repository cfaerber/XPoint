OpenXP - What's this about?

CrossPoint (XP) is a text-based mailer for various BBS nets like FidoNet, Z-Net and MausNet as well as Internet mail and news. In December 1999 the author of CrossPoint, Peter Mandrella, released CrossPoint v3.20ß sources to the public.
Since then many people worked on these sources - OpenXP has been born.

Versions of OpenXP

OpenXP/, 32 bits. Native Windows, Linux and DOS versions are available.

FreeXP (former OpenXP/16), is being developed at www.freexp.de since January 2002.
